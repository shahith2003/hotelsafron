About Us


Welcome to Hotel Safron, where authentic Indian flavors come to life! Located in the heart of Chennai, we take pride in serving the finest non-vegetarian delicacies, crafted with traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients. Our menu features a variety of mouthwatering dishes, from rich and aromatic curries to perfectly grilled kebabs and biryanis bursting with flavor. Whether you're craving the spicy indulgence of Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, the tenderness of Tandoori Chicken, or the creamy goodness of Butter Chicken, we have something to satisfy every palate. At Hotel Safron, we blend taste with hospitality, offering a warm and inviting ambiance perfect for family gatherings, friendly get-togethers, or a cozy meal with loved ones. Our chefs bring years of expertise, ensuring every dish is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage. Come dine with us and experience the best of Indian non-vegetarian cuisine!
